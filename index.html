Procurement

Procurement is the process of acquiring goods, services, or works from external sources for a business or organization. It involves identifying needs, selecting suppliers, negotiating contracts, and managing purchases.

Tender

A tender in procurement is a formal process where organizations invite suppliers or contractors to submit bids for providing goods or services. It ensures fairness, transparency, and competition among vendors.

Request for Proposal(RFP)

A Request for Proposal (RFP) in procurement is a formal document issued by an organization to invite suppliers or vendors to submit detailed proposals for providing goods or services. It outlines the project requirements, scope, specifications, and evaluation criteria to ensure transparency and fair competition.

Request for Quotation(RFQ)

A Request for Quotation (RFQ) in procurement is a formal document issued by a buyer to potential suppliers to obtain price quotations for specific goods or services. It typically includes detailed specifications, quantities, and delivery requirements.

Request for Information(RFI)

A Request for Information (RFI) in procurement is a formal process used by organizations to gather detailed information from potential suppliers about their products, services, capabilities, and solutions. It helps the buyer understand market offerings, evaluate vendor expertise, and identify possible options before making a purchasing decision.
